Light

Concetta Fazzino
"Light" , 2023
software: Procreate, Photoshop

“Because one man does not make light.”

- Nayt

This is the phrase that inspired me for this illustration. It can happen to everyone to lose their light, in these moments loneliness may seem like a safe place, but sometimes it is the safe places that are cages.
Remember to share your light and welcome the light of others.
